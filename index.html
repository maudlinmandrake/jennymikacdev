I've been tinkering around on the web since I was a wee one of 11, teaching myself how to code with a book on HTML and CSS, maintaining a newsletter on AOL Zine World, and customizing my home spaces on sites like Neopets and Livejournal. I fell off the wagon as the people around me encouraged my art abilities instead, probably because pursuing art seemed more noble than sitting in front of a computer all day.

I've been a stay-at-home mom for five years now, and my daughter will—finally!—be attending school full-time in a year. I've decided to use that year to throw myself completely and utterly into web development and coding. This page serves as a central hub as I learn in public.
